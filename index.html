Services Offered by Green Card Legal Help

    1. Family-Based Green Cards

    We assist individuals seeking permanent residency through family sponsorship, including spouses, children, parents, and siblings of U.S. citizens or lawful permanent residents. Our team ensures that all necessary documents are prepared and that you meet the requirements for eligibility.

    2. Employment-Based Green Cards

    If you re seeking to obtain a green card through employment, Green Card Legal Help can assist with navigating the complex requirements for labor certification, employer sponsorship, and visa categories, such as EB-1, EB-2, and EB-3.

    3. Adjustment of Status

    For individuals already in the U.S. on a visa, we offer expert legal assistance with the adjustment of status process, which allows you to apply for a green card without leaving the country.

    4. Green Card Renewals and Replacements

    Whether your green card has expired, been lost, or damaged, Green Card Legal Help offers quick and effective solutions for green card renewals and replacements.

    5. Resolving Green Card Issues

    If your green card application has been denied or delayed, or if you re facing other legal issues related to your permanent residency, we provide expert representation to resolve these challenges.
